Sponsors

Nexmo are giving us their entire beautiful London offices for completly free. In return they're looking for developes to try out their SMS texting API.

Indee are paying for both lunch and dinner on Sunday. In return they're simply looking to give back to the hackathon community and help grow it.

Sendgrid are paying for dinner on saturday. In return they're looking for developes to try out their emailing-as-a-service API.

Venue

BanterHack will be held in Nexmo's beautiful offices located only 4.20 seconds walk away from Old Street Station. The venue will be open all night and we'll be working really hard on making the sleeping arrangments as comfortable as possible.

Under 18s are more than welcome to attend, all we ask is that you let us know beforehand so that we can pair you up with someone who has been DBS checked and can take responsiblity over your safety :)

What to expect?

At BanterHack we believe in stress-free hacking environment, giving you the opportunity to learn, build and share cool stuff.

Don't worry! There's no forced theme or requirments. Feel free to come in and work on your own personal side projects or catch up on university work, just try and make something which you can share with us on Sunday.
